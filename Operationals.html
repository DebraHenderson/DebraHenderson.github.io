Operationals help you look good the moment you arrive!

Customer signs the contract.

The contract is important in many ways. Before we arrive we use the contract to know what to load into the trucks. When we put the their address into the GPS we call them to let them know we are on the way. Give them a little window of time fortraffic. When we arrive the customer signs the contract and pays any balance. Leave everythin in the truck until the contract is fully signed and paid. At the end of setup staff clean the inside of the inflatble quickly and sprays all the foot survaces and where the riders will touch the columns with Lycol Brand disinfectant. During this time, the checklist is completed and the 'walk around' is completed with the customer who receives their signed certificate and whistle.

Checklist

NJ reqires a checklist to make sure you complete all the setup procedures before the unit is allowed to be used by the customer and their guests. Our checklist is based on the ASTM world wide standard for the inflatable amusement ride industry. If you follow the checklist at every rental as you setup you and the kids will be safer. The checklist needs to be completed at every rental. You must do it while you are walking around the inflatable checking and confirming all tie downs, blower, tarp, cords and gfci are in good working order. Write any issues you notice on the section for it on the checklist and note it when you send in your payreport. Both s sides must be completed. The inspectors look for these documents and will know if you did it 'sitting in the truck' Checklists are legal documents.

Online training program

Just like you needed to complete the online traing program so does the customer - even if they are paying for supervison at a public rental. We have learned that having more people do the online training helps avoid accidents in the first place. Everyone is playing by the same rules and can appreciate what you are doing at public rentals. Customers must provide us with their 'test ID' and 'test certification number'. If they have not completed the 1/2 hour video program we may take the blower away until they do since they may not be able to supervise safely until they have compmeted the program.

Photographs

Photographs document your setup. We take several photos with the conpany smart phones once the unit has been conmpletle setup. We photograph the stakes or sand containers at each tie down, the blower in the back of the unit and the cords connected to the unit. We also take a photo for the website showing how attractive and clean the unit is. Photos would help you if there is ever an accident and we can proove it was setup correctly before we left or before we started a public rental. Take a look on our company FaceBook to see how public rentals are setup with the safety chairs and even gymastic mats.
