Air Castles Staff weekly pay report

Pay week starts on a Monday and ends on a Sunday. Type or select the ending Sunday date for your work week for this pay report.

In the following section please do the math do avoid delays!
  1. Provide your pay rate for supervision.
  2. Provide your pay rate for Setups and Take downs.
  3. Then add the number of hours you supervised and put that number on the line.
  4. Add the number of locations you setup/picked up for the whole week and put that on the line.
  5. Multiple the numbers to get totals for each type of work.
  6. Then add the two multiples together to get your week's total pay before any taxes are taken out. Write that on the final line.
